How this prevents overspending

Simply Pay reduces overspending by combining automatic limits, informative feedback, and purposeful friction. Smart Spend Caps set affordable guardrails based on your income and goals; Real‑time Nudges make the future cost of a purchase visible right when you're deciding; and the Delay & Reflect feature introduces a short, optional wait before discretionary purchases can clear. Together these patterns reduce impulse buys, increase awareness, and help money reach long‑term priorities instead of disappearing to quick impulses.
